What is Hadoop?

Hadoop is Opensource framework to store and process large sets of data in short time.

Organizations are collecting data from years. Now they want to extract useful information out of it, to remain competive.

Social,Mobile,Analytics and Cloud

The ability to extract high value from stored data to enable innovation and competitive gain is the purpose of Big Data analytics. Conducting analytics on large sets of data, business users and executives are able to see patterns and trends in performance, new relationships between data sets and potentially new sources of revenue.
